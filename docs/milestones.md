### Before 2026-07-31

- Confirm shortlisted status.
- Track development board shipment.
- Prepare board bring-up checklist.

### 2026-08 to 2026-11-01

- Port model inference and policy runtime to the target Qualcomm device.

- Record real robot demonstrations and convert them to a LeRobot-compatible dataset.

- Prepare online final submission package.

## Awards

### End of 2026
